Ballpark Matchups

Live batter vs. pitcher stats. Nothing else.

Ballpark Matchups delivers exactly what you need to know, exactly when you need it — and nothing else. Open the app, see the current batter, the pitcher, and the situational stats that matter right now. No graphics, no animations, no ads. Just clean, fast data pulled live from the MLB feed. Glance at it between pitches, put your phone down, and get back to watching the game.

Features

  • Live batter vs. pitcher matchup data, updated every 12 seconds
  • Situational splits: RISP, bases loaded, late & close, pitch count, and more
  • Current game stats for both batter and pitcher
  • Covers MLB and MiLB games
  • Detect your ballpark automatically, or browse today's games
  • No ads, no account required, no distractions

Privacy Policy

Ballpark Matchups does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app requests your location only to identify which ballpark you are at; this location is never transmitted off your device or stored anywhere. No analytics, no tracking, no account required.

Live game data is fetched from the publicly available MLB Stats API (statsapi.mlb.com) for personal, non-commercial use.
